üîê TOTP Authenticator

A secure, browser-based Time-based One-Time Password (TOTP) authenticator for managing your two-factor authentication codes without relying on mobile apps.

‚ú® Key Features
  • Browser-Based Security - All data stored locally in your browser, never sent to servers
  • NTP Time Synchronization - Ensures accurate time sync for reliable TOTP generation
  • OCR Secret Key Extraction - Paste screenshots to automatically extract secret keys or scan QR codes
  • Multiple Account Management - Add unlimited accounts with custom service names and identifiers
  • Visual Countdown Timer - Clear progress indicator showing when codes will refresh
  • Customizable Colors - Personalize each account with color coding for easy identification
  • Flexible Configuration - Support for 6/7/8 digit codes and 30/60 second periods
  • One-Click Copy - Instant copy to clipboard for quick authentication
  • Export/Import - Backup and restore your accounts (coming soon)
üöÄ How to Use
  1. Click "Add Account" to create a new TOTP entry
  2. Enter the service name and account identifier
  3. Add your secret key by:
    • Typing the secret key manually, OR
    • Taking a screenshot of the QR code or secret key area and pasting it (Ctrl+V) into the secret key field
  4. Customize the color, digits, and period if needed
  5. Click "Save Account" to start generating codes
  6. Click on any code to copy it to your clipboard
  7. Watch the progress bar to see when codes will refresh
üîí Security & Privacy
  • Local Storage Only - All secret keys remain in your browser's local storage
  • No Server Transmission - TOTP codes are generated client-side using cryptographic algorithms
  • Time Sync via NTP - Accurate time synchronization without exposing sensitive data
  • OCR Processing - Screenshot analysis happens locally in your browser
üí° Tips
  • Use the OCR feature to quickly add accounts from QR codes or secret key screenshots
  • Assign different colors to different service types for easier organization
  • Keep your secret keys secure - they provide access to your accounts
  • Verify the first few codes against the service's verification before removing other authenticators
